Member of a 2-person team whose task was to develop a desktop videogame. My main contributions were in creating and improving the concept and creating the UI elements.

The story is about a character that doesn’t like to eat vegetables nor to exercise. One day he got stuck in a dream full of candy, pastries and other sweet things. But the candy were evil, and he wanted to get out of that nightmare.
