It was requested to develop a project - with no restrictions - by starting from the words “urban walker”.

It was decided to go for a mobile application where users could share images and short videos about what they love about their city. Besides the app, it was also thought to have a different kind of interaction with other “urban walkers“ that used digital CIP/MUPI’s1 in strategic points of the city.

To help the definition of the scope of the project and its own development it was used different techniques for idea generation from E. Lupton’s “Graphic Design Thinking” <a href="#exercise" class="ref">1</a>.

After deciding the concept of the project, it was researched similar mobile applications that promoted photo and video sharing and it was also studied how they work and their in- terface. While doing that, it was sketched and designed some mockups of the app.

The final result was a mix between [Vine](http://vine.co/), [Instagram](http://instagram.com/) and [Yo App](https://www.justyo.co/) <a href="#wireframes" class="ref">2</a>.
